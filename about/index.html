About DaVeri

Last updated: 2026-02-13

What is DaVeri?

DaVeri.io is a SaaS platform that helps teams build and manage AI bots from one dashboard. It combines bot setup, conversation management, and credit-based usage controls in a single place.

Who it's for

DaVeri is designed for businesses and teams that want to launch AI-assisted customer interactions without building a custom backend. It fits use cases where teams need practical controls over bot behavior, message history, and usage limits.

How it works (high level)

You create an account, sign in, and configure bots in your dashboard. The platform uses Supabase for authentication and data services, with Cloudflare supporting infrastructure and security. Credits and plan limits define how much usage is available in your account.
