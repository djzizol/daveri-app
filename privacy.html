Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-13

1. Data Controller

DaVeri.io is the administrator (data controller) of personal data processed through the DaVeri.io platform. You can contact us at hello@daveri.io.

2. What Data We Collect

Account and authentication data

  • Email address.
  • User identifier (for example, UUID).
  • Authentication and session information provided through Supabase Auth and Google OAuth.

Usage and service data

  • Messages, conversation metadata, usage credits, and plan-related limits.
  • Dashboard activity needed to operate features and troubleshoot issues.

Technical data

  • IP address, browser type, device and system information, and approximate location derived from IP.
  • Security and operational logs.

Cookies and similar technologies

  • Essential cookies and session cookies required for authentication and basic platform operation.
  • Basic analytics technologies, where applicable.

3. Why We Collect Data

  • To create and maintain user accounts and log users in securely.
  • To provide the DaVeri.io dashboard, AI bot management, conversations, and credit-based usage controls.
  • To enforce usage limits and plan rules.
  • To monitor security, prevent abuse, and maintain service reliability.
  • To process payments and subscriptions if paid plans are enabled.

4. GDPR Legal Bases

  • Performance of a contract: providing the service you request after registration.
  • Legitimate interests: service security, abuse prevention, product improvement, and diagnostics.
  • Consent: where required, for non-essential cookies or similar technologies.

5. Processors and Third Parties

We use trusted service providers to operate DaVeri.io, including:

  • Google for OAuth authentication.
  • Supabase for authentication, database, and storage services.
  • Cloudflare for infrastructure, edge delivery, and security features.
  • Payment provider (for example Stripe) for billing and subscriptions, if applicable.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is generally stored until you delete your account or request deletion.
  • Service and security logs are retained for a limited and reasonable period needed for operations, security, and compliance.
  • Some records may be kept longer when required by law or to resolve disputes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Request restriction of processing or object to specific processing.
  • Receive your data in a portable format.
  • File a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

8. Deleting Your Account and Data

If account deletion is available in the dashboard, you can use that option. You can also request account and data deletion at hello@daveri.io.

9. Cookies

DaVeri.io uses cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential platform functionality.
  • Authentication and active sessions.
  • Basic analytics and performance measurement, where applicable.

10. International Transfers

Our providers may process data in multiple countries. When required, we apply appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

11. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact DaVeri.io at hello@daveri.io.
